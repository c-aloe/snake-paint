🧠 Design Philosophy Behind This Progression
Levels 1–3 → Learn Control

Small maps

Moderate growth

No time pressure

Introduces partial completion %

Levels 4–6 → Scale & Pressure

Larger areas

Speed increase

Time limits introduced

Lower completion requirement (reduces frustration)

Level 7 → Growth Disabled Twist
growth_enabled = false
snake_width_growth_speed = 0

This forces route planning instead of blob coverage.

Levels 8–10 → Controlled Chaos

High speed

Large maps

Growth + pressure

Reveal multiplier increased for visual satisfaction



Levels 6–10 are perfect places to introduce:
obstacles = [
	Vector2(5, 5),
	Vector2(5, 6),
	Vector2(5, 7)
]
Or generate them procedurally per level.

📈 Difficulty Curve Notes

If you want smoother scaling, increase only one variable at a time:

Map size

Speed

Growth

Time pressure

Completion %

Never spike more than two at once.
